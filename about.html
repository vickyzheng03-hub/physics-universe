About this project

Physics Universe was created as a Personal Project to help junior high school students understand physics more clearly.

Purpose

Many students find physics difficult because the ideas feel abstract and the explanations in textbooks can be too heavy. This website was designed to make physics easier with simple language, formulas, examples, and quizzes.

Target audience

The target audience is junior high school students who need a clearer and more visual way to learn physics.

Success features

  • Simple navigation
  • Student-friendly language
  • At least 5 physics topics
  • Formulas with symbol meanings
  • Worked examples
  • Interactive quizzes
  • Feedback section for evaluation

Why the name changed

The website title is now Physics Universe to make the project feel broader, more memorable, and more engaging for student users.
